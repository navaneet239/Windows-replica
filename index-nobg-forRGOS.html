WIN REPLICAS

By Navaneet

Win Replicas lets you experience the look and feel of classic Windows operating systems right from your web browser. It covers Windows 95, XP, 7, 10, and even the latest, Windows 11. Using web applications, it mimics the desktop of these systems, giving you a nostalgic trip through the evolution of Windows and its familiar dialog boxes that defined each Windows version. It's like a time machine for your computer, bringing back the vibes of these iconic interfaces.
